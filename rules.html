Learn the game step by step

Basic Rules of Baseball

1

Teams & Players

Two teams of 9 players each take turns batting and fielding.

2

Innings

A game has 9 innings. Each team bats once per inning.

3

Scoring Runs

Score by hitting the ball and running around all 4 bases.

4

Strikes & Balls

3 strikes = out. 4 balls = walk to first base.

5

Outs

3 outs ends a team's batting turn.

6

Winning

The team with most runs after 9 innings wins.

How to Play - Step by Step

1

Pitching

The pitcher throws the ball toward home plate where the batter stands.

2

Batting

The batter tries to hit the ball into the field of play.

3

Running

If the ball is hit, the batter runs toward first base.

4

Fielding

Defensive players try to catch the ball or tag runners out.

5

Scoring

A run scores when a player circles all bases and touches home plate.
